Analyze campaigns and provide optimization recommendations. Context: $ARGUMENTS

Use the Synter API to:
1. Pull current campaign performance across platforms
2. Analyze keyword quality scores and identify waste
3. Compare ad creative performance
4. Evaluate budget allocation efficiency
5. Check audience targeting effectiveness

Present a prioritized optimization plan:
- Quick wins (changes that can be made immediately)
- Budget reallocation recommendations with expected impact
- Keyword additions/removals
- Creative refresh suggestions
- Bid strategy adjustments
